## Platform-Specific Adaptations

### Facebook Feed
- **Format:** Carousel (Primary), Single Image
- **Specs:** 1080x1080px (1:1) or 1200x628px (1.91:1)
- **Text:** Primary text (125 chars), Headline (40 chars)
- **CTA:** "Learn More", "Sign Up", "Shop Now"

### Instagram Feed
- **Format:** Carousel, Single Image, Reels
- **Specs:** 1080x1080px (1:1) or 1080x1350px (4:5)
- **Caption:** Up to 2,200 chars (optimal: 150-300)
- **Hashtags:** 11-15 (mix branded, local, trending)

### Instagram Stories
- **Format:** Vertical video/image
- **Specs:** 1080x1920px (9:16)
- **Duration:** 15 seconds maximum per story
- **Interactive:** Polls, questions, countdowns, stickers

### TikTok
- **Format:** Vertical video only
- **Specs:** 1080x1920px (9:16)
- **Duration:** 15-60 seconds (optimal: 15-30s)
- **Style:** Authentic, trending audio, behind-the-scenes

### Google Display
- **Format:** Responsive Display Ads
- **Specs:** Multiple sizes provided
- **Assets:** Logo, images, headlines, descriptions
- **Optimization:** Google auto-combinations

### YouTube
- **Format:** In-stream (skippable), Bumper (6s)
- **Specs:** 16:9 (landscape) or 1:1 (square)
- **Duration:** 6s (bumper), 15-30s (in-stream)
- **Style:** Short, punchy, clear CTA

## Brand Guidelines

### Color Palette
**Primary:**
- Brand Blue: #2C5F8D (trust, professional)
- Brand Green: #8FCB9B (growth, calm)

**Accent:**
- Warm Orange: #E8985E (energy, coffee)
- Soft Yellow: #F4E9CD (natural light)

**Neutral:**
- Charcoal: #333333 (text)
- Light Grey: #F5F5F5 (background)
- White: #FFFFFF (space)

### Typography
**Headlines:** Montserrat or Open Sans (Bold, 600-700)
**Body:** Lato or Roboto (Regular, 400)
**Accent:** Playfair Display (for quotes, luxury feel)

### Logo Usage
- Minimum size: 100px width
- Clear space: Logo height × 0.5
- Don't stretch or distort
- Use on light or dark backgrounds with proper contrast

### Photography Style
**Lighting:** Natural, bright, warm
**Color:** Saturated but natural, slightly warm
**Composition:** Rule of thirds, depth of field
**Subjects:** Diverse, authentic, real members (when possible)
**Vibe:** Professional but approachable, not stock-photo perfect

### Video Style
**Frame rate:** 24fps or 30fps
**Movement:** Smooth, handheld for authenticity, gimbal for tours
**Audio:** Clear, no background noise
**Subtitles:** Always include (Vietnamese)
**Music:** Royalty-free, upbeat but not distracting

## Testing Strategy

### A/B Test Variables
1. **Visuals:** Photo vs. graphic, person vs. space, warm vs. cool colors
2. **Headlines:** Question vs. statement, benefit vs. feature
3. **CTAs:** "Learn More" vs. "Sign Up" vs. "Get Quote"
4. **Formats:** Carousel vs. single image vs. video
5. **Length:** 15s vs. 30s vs. 60s video

### Test Schedule
- **Week 1-2:** Creative variation test (3-5 versions)
- **Week 3-4:** Format test (carousel vs. video)
- **Month 2:** CTA test
- **Month 3:** Length test

### Success Metrics
- **CTR:** ≥ 2% (Facebook), ≥ 3% (Google Search)
- **Engagement rate:** ≥ 5% (social)
- **Video completion rate:** ≥ 50% (15s), ≥ 30% (30s)
- **Conversion rate:** ≥ 3% (landing page)
- **CPA:** ≤ 150,000 VND
