## 12. Optimization Schedule

### Week 1: Launch & Monitor
- Launch all campaigns
- Monitor impressions, CTR, CPC daily
- Add negative keywords as discovered
- Check search query reports

### Week 2-4: Initial Optimization
- Pause keywords with CTR < 1%
- Increase bids on top performers
- Test 2-3 ad variations per ad group
- Refine location targeting based on data

### Month 2-3: Advanced Optimization
- Implement ad scheduling (dayparting)
- Adjust device bids (mobile vs desktop)
- Test different bid strategies
- Expand winning keywords
- Refine audiences for display

### Month 4+: Scale & Expand
- Scale top performers
- Test new ad formats
- Expand to new locations (if performing well)
- Implement advanced bid strategies (tCPA, tROAS)
