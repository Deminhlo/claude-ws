# Giai đoạn 1: Chiến Lược Facebook Ads Chi Tiết

## Bối cảnh
- **Địa điểm:** 181 Trần Quốc Vượng, Cầu Giấy, Hà Nội
- **Mục tiêu:** Thu hút khách hàng tiềm năng, tăng nhận diện thương hiệu
- **Thị trường:** Freelancers, startups, remote workers tại Hà Nội

## 1. Campaign Objectives

### 1.1 Brand Awareness Campaign
**Mục tiêu:** Tăng nhận diện thương hiệu với đối tượng rộng

**KPIs:**
- Reach: 30,000+ người/tháng
- Frequency: 2-3 lần/người
- Cost per 1,000 impressions (CPM): ≤ 30,000 VND

**Cài đặt:**
- Campaign objective: Brand Awareness
- Buying type: Auction
- Optimization: Reach
- Delivery: Standard

### 1.2 Traffic Campaign
**Mục tiêu:** Thu hút traffic đến website/landing page

**KPIs:**
- Link clicks: 1,000+/tháng
- Cost per click (CPC): ≤ 5,000 VND
- Landing page views: 500+/tháng
- CTR: ≥ 2%

**Cài đặt:**
- Campaign objective: Traffic
- Conversion location: Website
- Optimization: Landing page views
- Delivery: Standard

### 1.3 Lead Generation Campaign
**Mục tiêu:** Thu thập leads qua Facebook Lead Forms

**KPIs:**
- Leads: 60+/tháng
- Cost per lead (CPL): ≤ 150,000 VND
- Lead quality rate: ≥ 60%
- Qualified leads: 36+/tháng

**Cài đặt:**
- Campaign objective: Lead Generation
- Conversion location: Instant Forms
- Optimization: Leads
- Delivery: Standard

### 1.4 Conversion Campaign
**Mục tiêu:** Chuyển đổi thành trial sign-ups

**KPIs:**
- Conversions: 25+/tháng
- Cost per acquisition (CPA): ≤ 500,000 VND
- Conversion rate: ≥ 5%

**Cài đặt:**
- Campaign objective: Sales/Conversions
- Conversion event: Complete registration
- Optimization: Conversions
- Delivery: Standard

## 3. Ad Formats & Creative Strategy

### 3.1 Carousel Ads (Primary Format)
**Sử dụng cho:** Showcase không gian, amenities, packages

**Specs:**
- Cards: 5-10
- Image size: 1080x1080px (1:1)
- Text: Primary text (125 chars), Headline (40 chars), Description (30 chars)
- CTA: "Learn More", "Sign Up", "Get Quote"

**Content ideas:**
- Tour không gian (Card 1-5: Different areas)
- Packages comparison (Day pass, Week pass, Month pass)
- Amenities showcase (Wifi, Cafe, Meeting rooms)
- Member testimonials

### 3.2 Single Image Ads
**Sử dụng cho:** Promotions, announcements, single messages

**Specs:**
- Image size: 1080x1080px or 1200x628px
- Text: Primary text (125 chars), Headline (40 chars)
- CTA: "Learn More", "Contact Us", "Sign Up"

**Content ideas:**
- Special promotions (50% off first day)
- Event announcements (Networking Night)
- Quote graphics
- Feature highlights

### 3.3 Video Ads (High Priority)
**Sử dụng cho:** Brand storytelling, testimonials, behind-the-scenes

**Specs:**
- Length: 15-60 seconds (sweet spot: 30s)
- Format: MP4, MOV
- Resolution: 1080x1080 (1:1) or 1080x1920 (9:16)
- File size: Max 4GB
- Captions: Required

**Content ideas:**
- "A Day at Sol Cafe" (30-60s)
- Member testimonials (15-30s)
- Behind-the-scenes (15s)
- Quick tips (15-30s)
- Event recaps (30s)

### 3.4 Collection Ads (Instagram)
**Sử dụng cho:** Product showcase, instant experience

**Specs:**
- Cover image: 1080x1080px
- 3-4 items
- Instant Experience fullscreen

**Content ideas:**
- "Explore Sol Cafe" - Virtual tour
- "Packages & Pricing" - All options
- "Amenities" - Features showcase

### 3.5 Lead Form Ads
**Sử dụng cho:** Direct lead generation

**Form fields:**
- Name (Required)
- Phone number (Required)
- Email (Optional but recommended)
- Interest (Dropdown: Day pass, Week pass, Month pass, Private office)
- When to start (Dropdown: Today, This week, This month, Just exploring)

**Form tips:**
- Keep under 5 fields
- Use contextual questions
- Add privacy disclaimer
- Follow up within 15 minutes

## 5. Placement Strategy

- **Reason:** High engagement, visual storytelling
- **Formats:** Carousel, Video, Single image
- **Optimization:** All placements, focus on Feed

### Facebook/Instagram Stories (15% budget)
- **Reason:** High reach among younger audience
- **Formats:** Video, Image
- **Optimization:** All placements, focus on Stories

### Facebook/Instagram Reels (10% budget)
- **Reason:** Viral potential, organic reach
- **Formats:** Vertical video (9:16)
- **Optimization:** All placements, focus on Reels

### Right Column (5% budget)
- **Reason:** Cheap clicks, retargeting
- **Formats:** Single image
- **Optimization:** Desktop only

### Messenger (Optional)
- **Reason:** Direct communication
- **Format:** Sponsored messages
- **Use for:** Retargeting, event reminders

## 8. Testing & Optimization Plan

### Week 1-2: Launch & Initial Testing
- Launch all campaigns with minimum budget
- Test 3-5 creatives per ad set
- Test 2-3 audiences per objective
- Monitor CPM, CPC, CTR, CPC

### Week 3-4: Analysis & Optimization
- Pause underperforming ads (CTR < 1%, CPC > 10K)
- Scale winning ads (increase budget 20-30%)
- Test new creative variations
- Refine audiences based on performance

### Month 2-3: Continuous Optimization
- A/B test headlines, images, CTAs
- Test new audiences
- Implement retargeting campaigns
- Optimize based on CPA, ROAS

### Month 4+: Scale & Expand
- Scale winning campaigns
- Test new formats (Stories, Reels)
- Expand to new audiences
- Implement dynamic creative testing

## 9. Key Metrics to Track Weekly

### Brand Awareness
- Reach, Impressions, Frequency
- CPM, CPC

- Link clicks, Landing page views
- CTR, CPC
- Bounce rate, Time on site

### Lead Generation
- Leads, Cost per lead
- Lead quality score
- Conversion rate (lead → customer)

### Conversion
- Conversions, Cost per acquisition
- Conversion rate
