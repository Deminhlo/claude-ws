## 4. Seasonal Search Trend Analysis

### Monthly Seasonal Patterns

**Peak Seasons (High Demand)**
- **October-December:** +30-40% increase
  - Reason: Year-end parties, corporate conferences, product launches
  - Keywords: "thuê sảnh tiệc cuối năm", "hội nghị tổng kết năm"
- **January-March:** +25-35% increase
  - Reason: New year planning, training programs, kick-off events
  - Keywords: "thuê địa điểm khai trương", "đào tạo nhân viên năm mới"
- **September:** +40% increase (academic year start)
  - Reason: University events, student workshops, educational programs
  - Keywords: "địa điểm workshop sinh viên", "training cho sinh viên"

**Shoulder Seasons (Medium Demand)**
- **April-May:** +10-15% increase
  - Reason: Spring events, team building activities
  - Keywords: "team building Hà Nội", "workshop kỹ năng mềm"
- **August:** +15-20% increase
  - Reason: Summer workshops, training intensives
  - Keywords: "khóa đào tạo hè", "workshop mùa hè"

**Low Seasons (Reduced Demand)**
- **June-July:** -20-30% decrease
  - Reason: Summer vacation, holidays, fewer business events
- **November (mid-month):** -15% decrease
  - Reason: Pre-holiday planning slowdown

### Quarterly Content Planning Recommendations
- **Q1:** Focus on new year planning, training programs
- **Q2:** Emphasize team building, spring workshops
- **Q3:** Target summer training, educational programs
- **Q4:** Prioritize year-end events, corporate parties
