## 🎓 KEY LEARNINGS & BEST PRACTICES

### What Works (Industry Benchmarks)

**Content:**
- Carousel ads outperform single images 2x
- Video testimonials have highest engagement
- User-generated content builds trust fastest

**Messaging:**
- Clear value proposition beats clever copy
- Social proof increases conversion
- Urgency drives action
- Specific numbers outperform vague claims

**Targeting:**
- Lookalike audiences have 2x better ROI
- Narrow targeting beats broad
- Custom audiences convert best

**Conversion:**
- Fewer form fields = Higher completion
- Payment confirmation increases booking
- Follow-up within 5min doubles conversion
