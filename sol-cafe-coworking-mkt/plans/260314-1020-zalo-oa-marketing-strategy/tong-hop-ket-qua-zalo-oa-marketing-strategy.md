## ⚠️ RISK MANAGEMENT

### High-Risk Areas

**Risk 1: Zalo OA Verification Delays**
- **Probability:** Medium
- **Impact:** High (cannot launch Phase 2-5)
- **Mitigation:** Apply early, prepare all documents, follow up weekly
- **Contingency:** Start với Generic OA, upgrade later

**Risk 2: Low Ad Performance**
- **Probability:** Medium
- **Impact:** High (wasted budget)
- **Mitigation:** Start small budget, test extensively, scale winners
- **Contingency:** Shift budget to organic content, referral marketing

**Risk 3: Mini App Development Overruns**
- **Probability:** High
- **Impact:** Medium (delay conversion optimization)
- **Mitigation:** MVP approach, strict scope control, experienced developer
- **Contingency:** Phase 2 features deprioritized, focus on chatbot booking

**Risk 4: Staffing Constraints**
- **Probability:** Medium
- **Impact:** Medium (slow response times)
- **Mitigation:** Cross-train staff, set up automation, hire freelancer support
- **Contingency:** Reduce customer service hours temporarily

**Risk 5: Budget Overrun**
- **Probability:** Low
- **Impact:** Medium (incomplete phases)
- **Mitigation:** 10% contingency buffer, weekly budget reviews
- **Contingency:** Prioritize phases, defer nice-to-have features

## 🎓 LEARNINGS & BEST PRACTICES

### What Works (Based on Industry Benchmarks)

**Content:**
- Carousel ads outperform single images by 2x
- Video testimonials have highest engagement
- User-generated content builds trust fastest
- Educational content gets shared most

**Messaging:**
- Clear value proposition beats clever copy
- Social proof (testimonials) increases conversion
- Urgency (limited time) drives action
- Specific numbers (prices, dates) outperform vague claims

**Targeting:**
- Lookalike audiences have 2x better ROI
- Narrow targeting beats broad (higher CTR)
- Custom audiences (website visitors) convert best
- Exclusions matter as much as inclusions

**Conversion:**
- Fewer form fields = Higher completion
- Payment confirmation increases booking certainty
- Follow-up within 5min doubles conversion
- Remarketing recovers 30% lost leads
