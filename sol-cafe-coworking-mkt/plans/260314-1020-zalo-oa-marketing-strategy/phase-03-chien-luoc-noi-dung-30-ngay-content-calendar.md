## KEY INSIGHTS

### Tại sao Content Strategy quan trọng?

1. **Top-of-mind awareness:** Khách hàng nhớ đến Sol Cafe khi cần sự kiện
2. **Social proof:** Feedback khách hàng tăng uy tín
3. **Value add:** Tips/helpful content tạo goodwill
4. **Algorithm favor:** Consistent posting → Higher reach
5. **Conversion driver:** Content → Traffic → Booking

### Content Pillars (Trụ cột nội dung)

| Pillar | Mô tả | Tỷ lệ | Ví dụ |
|--------|-------|-------|-------|
| **Space Showcase** | Ảnh/video không gian đẹp | 30% | Tour không gian, setup event |
| **Social Proof** | Feedback, review, testimonials | 25% | Khách hàng review, after photos |
| **Educational** | Tips, guides, checklists | 20% | Tips tổ chức workshop, checklist |
| **Promotional** | Ưu đãi, khuyến mãi, booking CTA | 15% | Giảm giá, last-minute deals |
| **Behind the Scenes** | Team, process, culture | 10% | Nhân viên, setup process |

### Best Posting Times trên Zalo

| Thời gian | Reach | Engagement | Khuyên dùng |
|-----------|-------|------------|-------------|
| **8:00-9:00** | Trung bình | Cao | ✅ Weekdays |
| **12:00-13:00** | Cao | Trung bình | ✅ Weekdays |
| **17:00-18:00** | Rất cao | Rất cao | ⭐ BEST TIME |
| **20:00-21:00** | Cao | Cao | ✅ Every day |

**Optimal:** 3-4 posts/tuần vào 17:00-18:00 hoặc 20:00-21:00

## REQUIREMENTS

### Functional Requirements

1. **Content Calendar:** 30-day plan với 12-15 posts
2. **Content Mix:** Đa dạng 5 pillars (không trùng lặp quá 2 days liên tiếp)
3. **Visual Assets:** Ảnh/video quality cao cho mỗi post
4. **Captions:** Engaging, CTA-clear, hashtags relevant
5. **Scheduling Tools:** Use Zalo OA scheduling feature
6. **A/B Testing:** Test 2-3 variations để tìm best performer

### Non-Functional Requirements

1. **Consistency:** Post 3-4x/week, same time slot
2. **Quality:** No spelling errors, high-res images
3. **Brand Voice:** Friendly, professional, helpful
4. **Engagement:** Reply to comments within 2h
5. **Adaptability:** Flexibility để adjust dựa trên performance

**Define Content Pillars cho Sol Cafe:**

1. **Space Showcase (30%)**

### Step 4: Design Visual Assets (Day 33-35)

**Image Specifications:**

| Type | Size | Format | Notes |
|------|------|--------|-------|
| **Single image** | 1080x1080px | JPG/PNG | High quality, < 2MB |
| **Carousel** | 1080x1080px/slide | JPG/PNG | 3-5 slides max |
| **Video** | 1080x1920px (9:16) | MP4 | < 30s, < 10MB |

**Design Guidelines:**

1. **Brand Colors:**
   - Primary: #2E7D32 (Xanh lá)
   - Secondary: #FFA000 (Vàng gold)
   - Accent: #FFFFFF (Trắng)
   - Background: #F5F5F5 (Xám light)

2. **Typography:**
   - Headlines: Montserrat Bold (24-32pt)
   - Body: Open Sans Regular (14-18pt)
   - CTA: Open Sans Bold (16-20pt)

3. **Image Style:**
   - Bright, well-lit photos
   - People-focused (show energy)
   - Clean, uncluttered
   - Consistent filter/grade

## RISK ASSESSMENT

### Potential Issues

**Risk 1: Low engagement on posts**
- **Nguyên nhân:** Content không relevant, posting time wrong
- **Mitigation:** A/B test posting times, survey audience interest
- **Contingency:** Shift content mix based on data

**Risk 2: Running out of content ideas**
- **Nguyên nhân:** Limited angles, repetitive content
- **Mitigation:** Content brainstorming sessions, user-generated content
- **Contingency:** Repurpose top-performing old content

**Risk 3: Visual quality inconsistent**
- **Nguyên nhân:** Rush production, lack of design resource
- **Mitigation:** Create templates, batch production, hire designer
- **Contingency:** Use stock photos/videos temporarily

**Risk 4: Time management - too much manual work**
- **Nguyên nhân:** Each post takes too long to create
- **Mitigation:** Create templates, batch production, scheduling tools
- **Contingency:** Reduce frequency temporarily (2-3 posts/week)
