## RISK ASSESSMENT

**Rủi ro chính:**
1. **Low engagement:** Content không thu hút → Solution: A/B test content types
2. **High CPC giá quá cao:** Competition cao → Solution: Target niche segments
3. **Technical issues:** Chatbot lỗi → Solution: Manual fallback support
4. **Budget overrun:** Ads inefficiency → Solution: Daily monitoring & optimization
