## 1. SEO Tools Stack

### 1.1 Essential SEO Tools

**Free Tools:**

| Tool | Purpose | URL | Cost |
|------|---------|-----|------|
| **Google Search Console** | Search performance, indexing, errors | search.google.com/search-console | Free |
| **Google Analytics 4** | Traffic analytics, user behavior | analytics.google.com | Free |
| **Google Business Profile** | Local SEO, reviews, insights | business.google.com | Free |
| **Google Trends** | Keyword trends, seasonality | trends.google.com | Free |
| **Google Keyword Planner** | Keyword research (basic) | ads.google.com/home/tools/keyword-planner | Free |
| **PageSpeed Insights** | Performance testing | pagespeed.web.dev | Free |
| **Mobile-Friendly Test** | Mobile usability | search.google.com/test/mobile-friendly | Free |
| **Rich Results Test** | Schema markup validation | search.google.com/test/rich-results | Free |
| **Screaming Frog (Free)** | Technical SEO audit (500 URLs limit) | screamingfrog.co.uk/seo-spider | Free (100% up to 500 URLs) |

**Paid Tools (Recommended):**

| Tool | Purpose | Monthly Cost | Priority |
|------|---------|--------------|----------|
| **Ahrefs** | Backlink analysis, keyword research, competitor research | $129+ | 🔴 Critical |
| **Semrush** | All-in-one SEO suite | $129+ | 🔴 Critical |
| **Moz Pro** | DA tracking, keyword research, on-page SEO | $99+ | 🟠 High |
| **BrightLocal** | Local SEO, citations, reviews | $39+ | 🟠 High |
| **Screaming Frog** | Technical SEO audit (unlimited) | $199/year | 🟡 Medium |

**Minimum Viable Stack (Budget-Friendly):**
- Google Search Console ✅
- Google Analytics 4 ✅
- Google Business Profile ✅
- Ahrefs OR Semrush (choose one)
- BrightLocal (for local SEO)
