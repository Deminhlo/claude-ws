## 1. Link Building Strategy Overview

### Goals (8 Weeks: Weeks 11-12 + Ongoing)
- 15+ quality backlinks
- 5+ edu/gov backlinks (bonus)
- 10+ local business backlinks
- Domain Authority increase: +10 points
- Referral traffic: 500+ visitors/month

### Backlink Quality Standards

**✅ Good Backlinks:**
- DA (Domain Authority) 30+
- Relevant to event/wedding/business industry
- DoFollow links
- Natural anchor text
- From indexed sites
- Real traffic (not PBNs)

**❌ Bad Backlinks:**
- DA < 20
- Unrelated niches (gambling, adult, etc.)
- NoFollow only
- Over-optimized anchor text
- From link farms
- Paid links (without rel="sponsored")

## 6. Outreach Workflow & Templates

### 6.1 Complete Outreach Sequence

**Step 1: Initial Outreach (Day 0)**
```
Subject: [Personalized subject line]

Hi [Name],

[Personalized opening showing you've done research]

[Value proposition - what's in it for them]

[Call to action - what you want]

[Sign-off]

[Your credentials]
```

**Step 2: Follow-up #1 (Day 3-5)**
```
Subject: Re: [Original subject]

Hi [Name],

Just following up on my previous email about [topic].

Did you have a chance to review? I'd love to hear your thoughts.

[Optional: Add new value/angle]

Best,
[Your Name]
```

**Step 3: Follow-up #2 (Day 7-10)**
```
Subject: Re: [Original subject] - One more thing

Hi [Name],

I know you're busy, but I wanted to share one more thing that might be relevant:

[New insight/resource/value]

Would [specific date/time] work for a quick call to discuss?

Best,
[Your Name]
```

**Step 4: Break-up Email (Day 14-21)**
```
Subject: Should I close the loop?

Hi [Name],

I haven't heard back, so I assume this isn't a priority right now.

I'll close my file on this, but feel free to reach out if [topic] becomes relevant in the future.

All the best,
[Your Name]
```

### 6.2 Outreach Best Practices

**Do's:**
✅ Personalize every email
✅ Keep emails short and focused
✅ Provide clear value upfront
✅ Use professional but friendly tone
✅ Follow up persistently (respectfully)
✅ Research recipient beforehand
✅ Optimize subject lines (open rates)
✅ Test different angles/offerings

**Don'ts:**
❌ Send generic bulk emails
❌ Ask for favors without giving value
❌ Use aggressive sales language
❌ Follow up too frequently (spam)
❌ Ignore personalized rejection
❌ Forget to proofread
❌ Use all caps or excessive punctuation
❌ Be overly formal or robotic

## Potential Issues & Solutions

**Issue 1: Low outreach response rate**
- Solution: Improve subject lines, personalize more, offer better value

**Issue 2: Getting NoFollow links only**
- Solution: Clarify link type upfront, negotiate DoFollow, or focus on referral traffic value

**Issue 3: Toxic backlinks acquired**
- Solution: Monitor regularly, use disavow tool, contact site owners to remove

**Issue 4: Competitors outranking**
- Solution: Analyze their backlinks, target same sites, create better content

**Issue 5: Time-consuming process**
- Solution: Use tools for automation, prioritize high-value targets, be patient

**Issue 6: Link building takes time to show results**
- Solution: Diversify strategies, focus on quick wins (directories) while building long-term assets (guest posts)
