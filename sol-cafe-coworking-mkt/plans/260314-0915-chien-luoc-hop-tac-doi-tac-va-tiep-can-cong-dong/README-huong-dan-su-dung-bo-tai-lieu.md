## ⚠️ LƯU Ý QUAN TRỌNG

### Legal Considerations

1. **Lawyer Review:** Have lawyer review all templates before use
2. **Contract Signing:** Always sign written agreements
3. **Data Privacy:** Comply with Vietnam data protection laws
4. **Intellectual Property:** Respect trademarks, logos

### Operational Best Practices

1. **Start Small:** Pilot with 1-2 partners before scaling
2. **Track Everything:** Use KPI sheet religiously
3. **Communicate:** Regular check-ins với partners
4. **Flexibility:** Adjust based on feedback

### Common Pitfalls to Avoid

❌ **Don't:**
- Sign long-term commitments upfront
- Overpromise on deliverables
- Ignore partner feedback
- Forget to follow up

✅ **Do:**
- Start with trial/pilot
- Underpromise, overdeliver
- Regular communication
- Persistent follow-up

## 🆘 SUPPORT & TROUBLESHOOTING

### Common Issues

**Issue:** Low response rate from outreach
**Solution:**
- A/B test email subject lines
- Personalize more
- Try different channels (phone, in-person)

**Issue:** Partners not active
**Solution:**
- Set clear expectations upfront
- Co-create marketing content
- Regular check-ins

**Issue:** Events poorly attended
**Solution:**
- Better pre-marketing
- Secure quality speakers
- Free food/drinks

**Issue:** Revenue not meeting targets
**Solution:**
- Review pricing strategy
- Focus on high-value segments
- Optimize conversion funnel
