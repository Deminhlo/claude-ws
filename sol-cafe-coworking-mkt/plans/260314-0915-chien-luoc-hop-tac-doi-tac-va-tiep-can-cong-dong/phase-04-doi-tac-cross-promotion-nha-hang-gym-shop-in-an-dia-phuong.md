## Cross-Promotion Models

### Model 1: Mutual Discount (Most Common)
- **Structure:** Cả hai bên offer discount cho customers của đối phương
- **Example:** Sol Cafe members 15% off tại Bún Bò Nam Bộ, restaurant customers 10% off day pass
- **Setup:** Members show Sol Cafe badge/card để nhận discount

### Model 2: Bundled Packages
- **Structure:** Tạo combo packages với partners
- **Example:** "Work hard, play hard" - Coworking month + Gym month = 5tr (giảm 1tr)
- **Setup:** Pre-paid bundle, revenue share theo %

### Model 3: Affiliate/Referral
- **Structure:** Commission cho mỗi referral
- **Example:** Sol Cafe gets 50k cho mỗi member sign up gym, gym gets 100k cho mỗi new customer
- **Setup:** Track với unique referral codes

### Model 4: Co-Branded Events
- **Structure:** Host joint events
- **Example:** "Wellness Wednesday" - Yoga session + Healthy lunch + Coworking day
- **Setup:** Split costs, share profits

## Partnership Agreement Structure

### Key Terms

**Điều khoản cơ bản:**
1. **Duration:** 6 months (renewable)
2. **Discount:** Xác định % discount cho cả hai directions
3. **Marketing:** Cả hai bên commit về social media posts, displays
4. **Tracking:** Monthly report về usage/redemption
5. **Termination:** 30 days notice

**Example Agreement (Bún Bò Nam Bộ):**
```
PARTNERSHIP AGREEMENT

Giữa:
- Sol Cafe Coworking (181 Trần Quốc Vượng)
- Bún Bò Nam Bộ (158 Trần Quốc Vượng)

Benefits:
1. Sol Cafe members: 15% off tại Bún Bò Nam Bộ
   - Show Sol Cafe badge/card
   - Valid: Mon-Fri, 11am-2pm (lunch hours)

2. Bún Bò Nam Bộ customers: 10% off Sol Cafe day pass
   - Show receipt từ Bún Bò Nam Bộ (same day)
   - Valid: Anytime

3. Marketing:
   - Sol Cafe: Feature Bún Bò Nam Bộ trên FB/IG (2 posts/tháng)
   - Bún Bò Nam Bộ: Display Sol Cafe flyers (near counter)

4. Tracking:
   - Monthly report về redemptions
   - Review sau 3 tháng

Duration: 6 months (Jul-Dec 2026)
Renewal: Auto-renew nếu cả two sides agree
Termination: 30 days written notice

Signed: _________    _________
Date:   _________    _________
```

## Risk Mitigation

| Rủi ro | Giải pháp |
|--------|-----------|
| Partners không active promote | Set clear expectations, co-create marketing content |
| Members not redeeming offers | Make offers easy to use, display prominently, regular reminders |
| Tracking difficulties | Simple system (badge show, code word), monthly manual count |
| Partner dissatisfaction | Regular check-ins, quarterly reviews, adjust terms if needed |
| Cannibalization (partners lose money) | Monitor partner revenue, ensure mutual benefit |
