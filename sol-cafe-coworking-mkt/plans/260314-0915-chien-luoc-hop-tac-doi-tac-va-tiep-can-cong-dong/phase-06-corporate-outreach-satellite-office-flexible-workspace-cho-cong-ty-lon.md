## Value Proposition Cho Large Corporations

### Problem Solved

**Pain Points của Large Companies:**
- Office space constraints (headcount growth faster than real estate)
- High Capex cho new offices (fit-out, furniture, deposit)
- Rigid leases (5-10 year commitments)
- Geographic limitations (employees live far from HQ)
- Project-based team needs (temporary, seasonal)
- Hybrid work models (part-time office, part-time remote)
- Meeting room shortages (peak hours)

### Sol Cafe Solution

**Flexible Workspace Models:**

#### Model A: Satellite Office
- **Use case:** Remote teams cần regular collaboration space
- **Capacity:** 20-50 seats
- **Commitment:** 6-12 months
- **Pricing:** 4-5tr/tháng/seat (vs 8-10tr traditional office)
- **Includes:**
  - Dedicated area (semi-private)
  - 24/7 access
  - Meeting room allocation (10-20 hours/tháng)
  - IT infrastructure (wifi, printer, scanner)
  - Reception services
  - Cleaning/maintenance

#### Model B: Project Hub
- **Use case:** Special project teams (3-6 months)
- **Capacity:** 10-30 seats
- **Commitment:** 3-6 months
- **Pricing:** 3.5-4.5tr/tháng/seat
- **Includes:** Same as Satellite Office
- **Flexibility:** Scale up/down monthly

#### Model C: Meeting Room Subscription
- **Use case:** Regular client meetings, training sessions
- **Capacity:** 10-20 people
- **Commitment:** 12 months
- **Pricing:** 5-8tr/tháng (unlimited hours)
- **Includes:**
  - Priority booking
  - Equipment (projector, video conferencing)
  - Catering options
  - Admin support

#### Model D: Employee Perks Program
- **Use case:** Employee benefit (flexible work options)
- **Capacity:** Unlimited employees
- **Commitment:** 12 months
- **Pricing:** 500k-1tr/tháng/employee (flat fee)
- **Includes:**
  - Day pass pool (employees can use anytime)
  - Meeting room allocation
  - Corporate rates
  - Monthly billing
  - Usage reporting

### Business Case (ROI Calculator)

**Traditional Office Cost (per seat/month):**
- Rent: 6-8tr
- Fit-out: 1-2tr (amortized)
- Deposit: 1tr (amortized)
- Utilities/Services: 500k-1tr
- **Total: 8.5-12tr/tháng/seat**

**Sol Cafe Flexible Workspace:**
- Satellite Office: 4-5tr/tháng/seat
- Project Hub: 3.5-4.5tr/tháng/seat
- **Savings: 40-60% vs traditional office**

**Additional Benefits:**
- No long-term lease commitment
- No Capex (fit-out, furniture)
- Scalability (scale up/down monthly)
- Prime location (Trần Quốc Vượng, Cầu Giấy)
- Turnkey solution (move-in ready)

### Bước 3: Meeting & Proposal (Tuần 5-7)

**Meeting Goals:**
- Understand company's workspace challenges
- Present tailored solution (not one-size-fits-all)
- Discuss pricing, terms, flexibility
- Build trust and relationship
- Identify decision-making process

**Proposal Structure:**
1. **Executive Summary:** Problem statement, proposed solution
2. **Company Analysis:** Understanding of their challenges
3. **Solution Design:** Tailored workspace model (Satellite/Project Hub/etc.)
4. **Pricing:** Transparent pricing with comparison to traditional office
5. **Case Studies:** Similar companies, results achieved
6. **Implementation:** Onboarding timeline, support structure
7. **Terms:** Contract length, flexibility, termination
8. **Next Steps:** Decision timeline, required information

### Bước 4: Trial & Pilot (Tuần 8-9)

**Pilot Program:**
- **Duration:** 1 month
- **Scope:** 10-20 seats for 1 team
- **Pricing:** Special pilot rate (20% discount)
- **Objective:** Prove value, collect feedback, build trust

**Pilot Deliverables:**
- Weekly check-ins with team lead
- Midpoint review (week 2)
- End-of-pilot report with metrics (usage, satisfaction, productivity)
- Proposal for expansion (if successful)

### Bước 5: Contract Negotiation (Tuần 10-11)

**Key Contract Terms:**
- **Commitment:** 6-12 months (with exit clause)
- **Seats:** Dedicated seats or pool-based
- **Pricing:** Monthly recurring revenue (MRR) model
- **Flexibility:** Scale up/down với 30-day notice
- **Services:** Included vs add-ons (meeting rooms, printing, etc.)
- **Termination:** 60-90 days notice
- **Payment:** Monthly invoicing, 30-day payment terms

**Negotiation Strategy:**
- Start với higher-tier pricing
- Offer discounts cho:
  - Longer commitment (12+ months)
  - Larger seat counts (50+ seats)
  - Multi-site agreements
- Be flexible on terms, not price

### Bước 6: Onboarding & Success (Tuần 12+)

**Onboarding Checklist:**
- [ ] Space setup (desk assignment, IT configuration)
- [ ] Access cards/keys distributed
- [ ] Team orientation (facility tour, policies)
- [ ] IT setup (wifi, printers, equipment)
- [ ] Meeting room booking setup
- [ ] Billing setup (contact person, invoicing schedule)
- [ ] Success manager assigned

**Success Metrics ( Quarterly Review):**
- Seat utilization rate
- Team satisfaction (quarterly survey)
- Meeting room usage
- Expansion requests (scale up/down)
- Renewal likelihood

## Risk Mitigation

| Rủi ro | Giải pháp |
|--------|-----------|
| Long sales cycle (6-12 months) | Start early, build pipeline, focus on quick wins (pilots) |
| Complex decision-making | Identify all stakeholders, build champions, tailor messaging |
| Price sensitivity | Demonstrate ROI with cost comparison calculator |
| Competition (Regus, WeWork) | Emphasize location, flexibility, local expertise, pricing |
| Large contracts = risk | Start smaller (pilots), prove value, expand gradually |
| Enterprise procurement complexity | Be flexible on terms, help navigate process |
