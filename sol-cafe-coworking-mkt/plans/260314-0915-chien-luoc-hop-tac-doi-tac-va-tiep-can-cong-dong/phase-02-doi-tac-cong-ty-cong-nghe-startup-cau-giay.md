## Value Proposition Cho Tech Companies

### B2B Package Options

#### Option A: Team Day Pass
- **Target:** Startup 5-20 người cần không gian họp/workshop
- **Price:** 200k/person/day (minimum 5 người)
- **Includes:**
  - Hot desk + high-speed wifi
  - 2 hours meeting room free
  - 10% discount on F&B
  - Invoice VAT cho công ty

#### Option B: Startup Monthly Pass
- **Target:** Team 5-15 người làm việc định kỳ
- **Price:** 3.5tr/tháng/person (commit 3 tháng)
- **Includes:**
  - Dedicated hot desk (same seat everyday)
  - 10 hours meeting room/tháng
  - 15% discount F&B
  - Locker riêng cho team
  - Event space 1 buổi/tháng (2h)

#### Option C: Corporate Flex Space
- **Target:** Team 10-30 người cần private area
- **Price:** Theo quote (tùy yêu cầu setup)
- **Includes:**
  - Dedicated area (private/suite)
  - 24/7 access
  - Custom setup (monitor, whiteboard...)
  - Dedicated account manager
  - Priority support

### Additional Benefits
- **Trial:** Free trial 1 ngày cho team (max 5 người)
- **Referral:** 20% commission khi referral công ty khác
- **Milestone discount:** 10% off khi renew >6 tháng
- **Event hosting:** Free venue cho launch party/kickoff (2h/năm)

## Chiến Lược Tiếp Cận Tech Companies

### Bước 1: Research & List Building (Tuần 1)
- [ ] LinkedIn search: "Software company", "Startup" + location "Cau Giay"
- [ ] Crunchbase search: Vietnam startups Series A-B
- [ ] Facebook Groups: "Hanoi Tech Community", "Startup Vietnam"
- [ ] Google Maps: "Công ty công nghệ" near Trần Quốc Vượng
- [ ] Build list 50 công ty với contact person

### Bước 2: Cold Outreach (Tuần 2-3)
**Sequence:**
1. **Email 1:** Intro + Value prop + Free trial offer
2. **Email 2:** (3 days later) Case study: "Team X boosted productivity Y% using Sol Cafe"
3. **Email 3:** (7 days later) Invitation to visit + Free coffee

**LinkedIn Strategy:**
- Connect with HR Manager/Office Manager/CEO
- Send personalized InMail
- Engage with their content first

**Phone Call:**
- Call HR/Office Manager (warm transfer từ email)
- Script: "Chào anh/chị, em gọi từ Sol Cafe Coworking..."

### Bước 3: Direct Visit (Tuần 4)
- [ ] Drop by offices (bring portfolio + pricing)
- [ ] Meet HR/Admin Manager in person
- [ ] Demo workspace: "Mời team ghé trải nghiệm 1 ngày free"

### Bước 4: Trial & Close (Tuần 5-6)
- [ ] Schedule trial day cho interested teams
- [ ] Assign dedicated host cho trial team
- [ ] Collect feedback + adjust quote
- [ ] Send proposal + contract

### Bước 5: Onboarding & Success (Tuần 7-8)
- [ ] Setup team workspace (desk arrangement, amenities)
- [ ] Train team về facility usage
- [ ] Setup monthly billing/invoicing
- [ ] Schedule check-in meeting (30 days)

## Risk Mitigation

| Rủi ro | Giải pháp |
|--------|-----------|
| Companies đã có office | Position as flexible workspace: part-time, overflow, project-based |
| Price sensitivity | Offer tiered pricing, startup-friendly terms, trial first |
| Decision-making chậm | Build relationship with multiple stakeholders, follow up consistently |
| Competition (Toong, CirCO) | Emphasize: location (Trần Quốc Vượng), cafe atmosphere, pricing |
