## Community Architecture

### Primary Platform: Zalo OA (Official Account)

**Tại sao Zalo?**
- 75%+ Vietnamese internet users use Zalo
- Higher engagement than Facebook trong Vietnam
- Better cho localized community
- Direct messaging, group chats
- Payment integration (cho future upgrades)

**Zalo Community Structure:**
- **Zalo OA (Official Account):** Official announcements, broadcasts
- **Zalo Groups:**
  - General group (all members): 500 members limit
  - Niche groups (themed): Freelancers, Developers, Startups, Students
  - Location-based: Cau Giay residents

### Secondary Platform: Facebook

**Facebook Page:**
- Public-facing content
- Reviews, ratings
- Event pages
- Ads/Promotions

**Facebook Groups:**
- Public group (5000+ members): Open community
- Private group (500 members): Exclusive cho residents/members

### Tertiary: Slack/Discord (Optional)

**Use Case:**
- Tech-focused members prefer Slack/Discord
- Better cho file sharing, integrations
- Professional networking
- Developer communities

**When to launch:**
- After 200+ active members
- When 30%+ members request it
- Resource allocation permits

## Monthly Events Strategy

### Event Types & Frequency

#### Weekly Events (Weekly, 52 events/năm)

**1. Monday Morning Coffee (Weekly)**
- **Time:** Monday 9-10am
- **Format:** Casual coffee networking
- **Goal:** Start week với energy, meet new members
- **Cost:** Free (coffee included)
- **Capacity:** 20-30 people

**2. Wednesday Workshop (Bi-weekly)**
- **Time:** Wednesday 6-7:30pm
- **Format:** Skill-building workshop
- **Topics:** Time management, freelancing, client management, tech skills
- **Speakers:** Members, external experts
- **Cost:** Free cho members, 50k cho non-members
- **Capacity:** 30-50 people

**3. Friday Social (Bi-weekly)**
- **Time:** Friday 5-7pm
- **Format:** Happy hour, games, networking
- **Activities:** Board games, trivia, movie nights
- **Cost:** Free (BYOB hoặc potluck)
- **Capacity:** 30-40 people

#### Monthly Events (Monthly, 12 events/năm)

**4. Monthly Member Mixer (Monthly)**
- **Time:** First Thursday 6-8pm
- **Format:** Networking + new member introductions
- **Activities:** Speed networking (3 min rounds), member spotlight
- **Cost:** Free cho members, 100k guests
- **Capacity:** 50-80 people

**5. Community Spotlight (Monthly)**
- **Time:** Third Tuesday 6-8pm
- **Format:** Member presentations/showcases
- **Content:** Members share projects, portfolios, success stories
- **Goal:** Celebrate wins, inspire others
- **Cost:** Free
- **Capacity:** 30-50 people

**6. Skill Share Session (Monthly)**
- **Time:** Last Saturday 2-4pm
- **Format:** Members teach skills to other members
- **Examples:** "How to use Figma", "Freelance pricing strategies", "SEO basics"
- **Speakers:** Volunteer members
- **Cost:** Free
- **Capacity:** 20-30 people

#### Quarterly Events (Quarterly, 4 events/năm)

**7. Quarterly Town Hall (Quarterly)**
- **Time:** First month of quarter (6-8pm)
- **Format:** State of community, roadmap, feedback
- **Content:** Achievements, upcoming events, member Q&A, voting
- **Cost:** Free (dinner included)
- **Capacity:** 50-100 people

**8. Seasonal Celebrations (Quarterly)**
- **Examples:** Tet party, Summer bash, Year-end gala
- **Format:** Party, food, entertainment
- **Cost:** Paid (ticketed hoặc sponsored)
- **Capacity:** 100-200 people

## Community Guidelines

### Code of Conduct

**Be Respectful:**
- No harassment, discrimination, hate speech
- Respect diverse opinions
- Constructive feedback only

**Be Relevant:**
- No spam, self-promotion (excessive)
- Stay on topic per group
- Use appropriate channels

**Be Professional:**
- No soliciting business (unless in designated channels)
- Respect privacy (no sharing personal info without consent)
- No NSFW content

**Consequences:**
- Warning (first offense)
- Temporary ban (1 week, second offense)
- Permanent ban (severe or repeat offenses)

---

## Crisis Management

### Potential Issues

**Issue 1: Low Engagement**
- **Solution:** Re-evaluate content, survey members, adjust strategy
- **Prevention:** Regular feedback loops, variety of content

**Issue 2: Toxic Behavior**
- **Solution:** Enforce guidelines, ban if necessary
- **Prevention:** Clear code of conduct, active moderation

**Issue 3: Spam/Self-Promotion**
- **Solution:** Remove posts, warn users
- **Prevention:** Clear rules, designated promo channels

**Issue 4: Member Attrition**
- **Solution:** Re-engagement campaigns, exit surveys
- **Prevention:** Regular value delivery, community bonding
