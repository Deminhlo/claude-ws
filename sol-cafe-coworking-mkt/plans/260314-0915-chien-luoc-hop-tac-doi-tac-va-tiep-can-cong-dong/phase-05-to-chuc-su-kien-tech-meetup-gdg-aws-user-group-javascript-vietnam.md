## Value Proposition Cho Tech Communities

### Đối với Community Organizers

**Venue Benefits:**
- **Free venue:** Free space cho meetups (2-3 hours, weekday evening hoặc Saturday)
- **Amenities included:**
  - High-speed wifi (1Gbps)
  - Projector + sound system
  - Whiteboards
  - 30-50 seating capacity
  - Air conditioning
- **Support:**
  - Setup assistance (chairs, tables, equipment)
  - Basic refreshments (water, tea)
  - Marketing support (share event trên Sol Cafe channels)
  - Post-event cleanup

**Additional Perks:**
- **Sponsorship matching:** Connect organizers với potential sponsors (tech companies)
- **Recurring slot:** Reserve monthly slot cho regular meetups
- **Co-branding:** "Hosted at Sol Cafe Coworking" trong event materials
- **Member perks:** 20% discount day pass cho community members

### Đối với Sol Cafe

**Marketing Benefits:**
- **Brand exposure:** 50-100 developers/community per event
- **Word-of-mouth:** Organic recommendations trong tight-knit communities
- **Content creation:** Photos, videos từ events cho social media
- **Talent pipeline:** Connect với potential resident members (developers, freelancers)
- **Networking:** Build relationships với tech leaders/influencers

**Revenue Potential:**
- Day pass conversions post-event
- Resident member signups
- Corporate leads (attendees work tại tech companies)
- Event sponsorship revenue (optional, cho larger events)

## Event Packages

### Package A: Community Meetup (Free)
**Target:** Regular monthly meetups (20-50 people)
**Includes:**
- Venue 2-3 hours (weekday 6-9pm hoặc Saturday 2-5pm)
- Basic setup (chairs, projector, sound)
- Water + tea
- Wifi access
**Requirement:**
- Minimum 20 attendees
- Public event (not private/corporate)
- Credit Sol Cafe sebagai venue host
- Share event photos/videos

### Package B: Workshop/Training (Paid)
**Target:** Hands-on workshops (15-30 people)
**Price:** 1-2tr/session (tùy requirements)
**Includes:**
- Everything in Package A
- Extended venue access (4-6 hours)
- Full-day access cho workshop
- Lunch/snacks (catering options)
- Printing materials (handouts, certificates)
**Add-ons:**
- Recording equipment: +500k
- Livestream setup: +500k
- Professional photographer: +1tr

### Package C: Conference/Hackathon (Custom)
**Target:** Large events (50-200 people)
**Price:** Custom quote
**Includes:**
- Full venue access (weekend)
- Full catering (coffee break, lunch)
- Event management support
- Sponsorship management
- Marketing support
**Partnership Model:**
- Revenue share (ticket sales sponsorship)
- Barter arrangement (promotion equity)

## Chiến Lược Outreach Cho Communities

### Bước 1: Research & Identification (Tuần 1-2)
**Activities:**
- [ ] Join Facebook groups của tất cả 15 communities
- [ ] Follow their Meetup.com, Eventbrite pages
- [ ] Identify organizers (moderators, admins)
- [ ] Research past events (frequency, attendance, venues)
- [ ] Note upcoming events (Q3 2026)

**Data Collection:**
```markdown
Community Profile Template:
- Name: GDG Hanoi
- Size: 15k members
- Organizer: [Name], [Contact]
- Past events: Android Study Jam, Cloud Next Extended
- Frequency: Monthly
- Typical attendance: 50-100
- Current venue: [Research]
- Pain points: [Research]
```

### Bước 2: Engagement & Relationship Building (Tuần 3-4)
**Pre-Outreach Engagement:**
- **Facebook Groups:**
  - Comment on posts (valuable contributions, not self-promo)
  - Answer questions (if knowledgeable)
  - Share relevant resources
- **Event Attendance:**
  - Attend 3-5 meetups (different communities)
  - Network với organizers, speakers, attendees
  - Observe venue setup, logistics, pain points
- **Social Media:**
  - Follow organizers trên LinkedIn, Twitter
  - Engage với their content

**Positioning Strategy:**
- Come as contributor, not salesperson
- Offer value first (venue sponsorship)
- Build trust over time

### Bước 3: Outreach & Proposal (Tuần 5-6)
**Initial Contact (Private Message):**
```
Hi [Organizer Name],

Em là [Your Name] từ Sol Cafe Coworking (181 Trần Quốc Vượng, Cầu Giấy).

Em đã follow [Community Name] một thời gian và ấn tượng với work của anh/chị - especially [specific event/post].

Em muốn propose partnership: Sol Cafe sponsor venue cho [Community Name] meetups - hoàn toàn free.

**What we offer:**
- Venue 2-3 hours (capacity 30-50)
- Wifi 1Gbps, projector, sound system
- Water, tea, basic refreshments
- Setup/cleanup support

**In return:**
- Credit "Hosted at Sol Cafe Coworking"
- Share event photos/videos
- Introduce Sol Cafe đến community members

Em có thể treat anh/chị coffee để discuss chi tiết không?

Thanks,
[Your Name]
```

**Follow-up Sequence:**
1. Initial DM (above)
2. If no reply trong 3 days → Comment on recent post + DM bump
3. If still no reply → Attend next meetup, approach in person

## Event Logistics & Operations

### Pre-Event Checklist (1 week before)
- [ ] Confirm date, time, agenda với organizer
- [ ] Setup equipment (projector, mic, sound check)
- [ ] Prepare refreshments (water, tea, coffee)
- [ ] Create event signage (Sol Cafe branding)
- [ ] Share event trên Sol Cafe social channels
- [ ] Send reminders to community (via organizer)

### Day-of Event Timeline
**2 hours before:**
- Setup venue (chairs, tables, projector)
- Test equipment
- Prepare refreshments

**During event:**
- Point person onsite (support organizer)
- Monitor wifi, equipment
- Refill refreshments
- Take photos/videos (with permission)

**Post-event (30 min):**
- Cleanup (with organizer help)
- Collect feedback
- Thank organizer + attendees

### Post-Event Follow-up (24-48 hours)
- [ ] Send thank-you email to organizer
- [ ] Share photos/videos (Google Drive link)
- [ ] Post recap trên social media (tag community)
- [ ] Ask organizer for testimonial
- [ ] Schedule next event (if recurring)

## Risk Mitigation

| Rủi ro | Giải pháp |
|--------|-----------|
| Low attendance events | Minimum attendee requirement (20 people), organizer promotion |
| Venue double-booked | Calendar system, lock in dates提前, clear communication |
| Equipment failure | Backup equipment (projector, mic), technician on-call |
| Organizer flake | Build strong relationships, have backup communities |
| Noise complaints | Schedule events trong non-peak hours, soundproofing |
