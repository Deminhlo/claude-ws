## Freelancer Resident Program

### Program Structure

#### Tier 1: Day Pass Resident
- **Target:** Casual freelancers (1-3 days/tuần)
- **Price:** 150k/day (vs 200k regular)
- **Benefits:**
  - 10% discount F&B
  - Access to freelancer Slack/Zalo group
  - Invited to monthly networking events
  - Profile on "Our Residents" board

#### Tier 2: Monthly Resident (Recommended)
- **Target:** Full-time freelancers (4-5 days/tuần)
- **Price:** 2.5tr/tháng (vs 3.5tr regular)
- **Benefits:**
  - Dedicated hot desk (same seat everyday)
  - 20% discount F&B
  - 5 hours meeting room/tháng
  - Locker storage
  - Priority booking for events
  - Profile + photo on website/Instagram
  - Resident badge/card
  - Invited to exclusive workshops

#### Tier 3: Premium Resident
- **Target:** High-earning freelancers ($2k+/month)
- **Price:** 4tr/tháng
- **Benefits:**
  - Everything in Monthly Resident
  - Private desk (not hot desk)
  - 10 hours meeting room/tháng
  - 30% discount F&B
  - Free month after 6 months
  - Featured in "Resident Spotlight" blog post
  - Free headshot photography session
  - Access to premium networking events

### Additional Perks (All Tiers)
- **Referral bonus:** 500k cho mỗi freelancer mới referrer
- **Milestone rewards:**
  - 3 months: Free Sol Cafe merch (t-shirt, tote bag)
  - 6 months: 1 week free
  - 12 months: 2 weeks free + featured article
- **Flexibility:** Pause membership 1 tháng (nếu traveling/vacation)

## Chiến Lược Outreach Cho Freelancers

### Bước 1: Community Engagement (Tuần 1-2)
**Facebook Groups Strategy:**
- **Week 1:** Join 10+ freelancer Facebook groups
- **Daily Activities:**
  - Post 1 valuable content/group (tips, resources, not self-promo)
  - Comment on 5-10 posts/day (genuinely helpful)
  - Answer questions related to coworking/remote work
- **Build credibility** trước khi pitch

**Content Examples:**
- "Tips: 5 apps giúp freelancer manage time hiệu quả"
- "Review: Các cafe tại Cầu Giấy phù hợp làm việc từ xa"
- "Share: Mình dùng tool gì để track client payments?"
- "Discussion: Freelancer nên làm việc ở cafe hay coworking space?"

### Bước 2: Soft Launch (Tuần 3)
- **Post in groups:** "Mình vừa tìm được workspace gem tại Cầu Giấy - Sol Cafe Coworking. Wifi 1Gbps, coffee ngon, atmosphere productivr. Có ai đã thử chưa?"
- **Direct message:** Freelancers active trong groups
- **Offer:** Free trial day cho interested members

### Bước 3: Resident Program Launch (Tuần 4)

## Risk Mitigation

| Rủi ro | Giải pháp |
|--------|-----------|
| Freelancers prefer working from home | Emphasize benefits: networking, separation of work/home, reliable infrastructure |
| Price sensitivity | Offer tiered pricing, free trial, referral discounts |
| Low engagement in community | Daily valuable content, exclusive events, feature residents regularly |
| Competition from home-cafes | Position as professional workspace, not just cafe |
| Seasonal fluctuations | Offer flexible pause/resume options |
