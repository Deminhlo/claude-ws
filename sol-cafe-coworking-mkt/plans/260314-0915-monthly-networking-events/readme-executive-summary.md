## 📊 Key Metrics & Targets

### Attendance (Số người tham dự)
- **Target:** 50 người/event (range: 30-80)
- **Show-up rate:** 70-80% của registered
- **Repeat attendance:** 40%+ quay lại events sau

### Revenue (Doanh thu)
- **Ticket revenue:** 11M VND (50 người × avg 220K)
- **Sponsorship:** 7M VND (1 Silver sponsor)
- **Total revenue:** 18M VND/event
- **Net profit:** 4.25M VND/event (31% ROI)

### Conversion (Chuyển đổi thành viên)
- **Interest capture:** 30% của attendees hỏi về coworking
- **Trial signup:** 10% của attendees đăng ký trial
- **Trial activation:** 80% của trials actually đến
- **Final conversion:** 8% của attendees → paying members
- **LTV per member:** 60M VND (5M/tháng × 12 tháng)

### Satisfaction (Sự hài lòng)
- **Target rating:** 4.5/5.0 stars
- **NPS (Net Promoter Score):** 40+
- **Would recommend:** 90%+ yes

## ⚠️ Key Risks & Mitigation

| Risk | Severity | Mitigation Strategy |
|------|----------|---------------------|
| Low attendance (< 30) | High | Aggressive early bird pricing, personal outreach |
| Speaker no-show | High | Backup speaker option, panel discussion pivot |
| Low conversion (< 5%) | High | Improve trial experience, personal follow-up |
| Budget overrun | Medium | 10% contingency buffer, sponsor revenue |
| Team burnout | Medium | Realistic frequency, hire additional help |

## 💡 Critical Success Factors

1. **Consistency:** Same format, same quality, every month (build trust)
2. **Community-first:** Events serve community, not just sales funnel
3. **Data-driven:** Measure everything, optimize continuously
4. **Team alignment:** Everyone knows roles, scripts, responsibilities
5. **Speaker quality:** Great content = repeat attendance
6. **Follow-through:** Post-event engagement is khi conversion happens
